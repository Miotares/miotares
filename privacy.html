1. General Information

Bitcoin Insight is a free app that displays public Bitcoin network data such as the current price, mempool status, and halving countdown. The app does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Data Collection and Usage

  • The app does not require user registration and does not access any personal information.
  • All displayed data (price, mempool, halving, etc.) is fetched from publicly available APIs.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking tools are used.

3. Permissions

Bitcoin Insight does not request any special permissions on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may retrieve public data from external sources (e.g., Bitcoin network APIs). These services are accessed anonymously without transmitting personal data.
